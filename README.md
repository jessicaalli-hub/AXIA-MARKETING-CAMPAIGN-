# AXIA-MARKETING-CAMPAIGN-
This report provides a detailed breakdown of the analytical process used to create the AXIA Digital Marketing Dashboard. The visualization tool used for this analysis was Power BI, and DAX measures were also employed. It covers the data preparation, transformation,visualization, and insights derived from the dashboard
The dataset contains marketing output data per month across several digital channels. Key fields include: Impressions, Clicks, Revenue (₦), Ad Spend (₦) , CTR (Click-Through Rate), ROI (Return on Investment), Campaign Name / Channel, and  Date.
The dataset was thoroughly checked and cleaned to ensure accuracy and reliability. Key preparation steps included:
• Verifying and correcting data types:The Campaign Datecolumn was originally stored as text. It was converted to the correct Date format using the Locale option to 
ensure proper time-based analysis.
• Checking for missing values and inconsistencies:The dataset was reviewed to identify and address any gaps, anomalies, outliers or irregular entries that could affect insights.
• Standardizing financial fields:Monetary values such as Spend and Revenue were reformatted and validated to maintain consistency and support accurate 
calculations.
Based on the analysis and dashboard visuals, February shows a clear drop across almost all major marketing metrics. Impressions fell drastically to 25M,compared to 91M in January which indicates reduced campaign exposure, possibly due to lower ad budget or fewer active campaigns.
Key insights indicate that Google Ads had the highest ad spend and contributed significantly to overall performance, January recorded the highest impressions (91M) and strong ROI (148K), Conversion trends showed spikes aligned with high-spend periods, Influencer Marketing had the lowest ad spend but can be optimized.
The top products by revenue include Cold Drink, Biscuits, Air Freshener,and Cooking Oil
